My Motivation For Outreachy?

I was always keen to learn about open source contribution. Back in the year 2019, a friend of mine has suggested me outreachy. I researched a lot about it and was all set but due to some circumstances I could not apply at that time. I had been waiting for a long time to be a part of the outreachy organization. And yessss!!!!! The day is finally here :)

Moving to, How I got selected as Outreachy Intern for Mozilla Firefox!!

Contribution Period!!

So, we had an Initial Application Round before Contribution Period. Where basic details were asked along with some essay type questions. After clearing the initial application round, I started exploring various open-source projects that were relevant to my interests. Due to my prior experience, I was looking for organizations that require knowledge of Javascript. I found Mozilla Firefox to be extremely aligned with my interests. I have been using Firefox for a very long time now and contributing to it was like a dream for me. I contacted my mentor Emma and asked for starter bugs. Thanks to her constant guidance, I was able to submit a few patches before the contribution deadline. Thankfully, the Outreachy organizers and Mozilla mentors found my contributions to be good enough to offer me an internship. I am very excited to embark on this beautiful journey for three months.  

The Way Ahead!

Right from week 1, I am into solving bugs. Currently, I am working on two bugs pertaining to the screenshot module of firefox (https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=1679313 and https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=1710635). I also had my first meeting this week where I introduced myself to my mentors Sam Foster and Emma Malysz and discussed plans for this and subsequent weeks. I hope to keep working at this pace and seek help from mentors whenever I am stuck. Hope to have a great learning experience along with great opportunities that come along with Outreachy.
